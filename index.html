Why now?

Belgian pension law demands transparency — at scale.

Every Belgian pension plan sponsor must provide every affiliated member with an Informatiefiche voor de Aanvullende Pensioenen (IDAP). This document must follow a standardized FSMA template, cover 11 mandatory sections, and be available in the member's language. Managing this manually for thousands of plans is no longer sustainable.

We are already building this solution at a major Belgian insurance group. We know the regulation. We know the edge cases. We deliver.

WAP

Wet Aanvullende Pensioenen

Every affiliated member must receive a standardized information sheet covering plan type, contributions, benefits, investment and exit rights.

FSMA

FSMA IDAP template

The Financial Services and Markets Authority prescribes a fixed section structure. Content must be traceable to the underlying pensioenreglement.

EU AI Act

AI Act transparency obligations

AI-generated regulatory documents require full auditability, confidence scoring, human oversight and documented governance — built into our architecture.

Art. 24 WAP

Return guarantees & disclosure

Contribution levels, guaranteed returns, and cost structures must be disclosed precisely — our extraction validates these numerically against source documents.

How it works

Five phases from document to compliant IDAP

Phase 01
🔍

Digitization

Mistral AI OCR ingests all pensioenreglementen — PDFs, scans, Word files — normalising them into a clean, structured knowledge base per pension scheme.

→
Phase 02
🧩

Extraction

OpenAI + Python extracts the relevant sections AS-IS, by section key. No paraphrasing — exact text captured per IDAP rubric, tagged with source page and paragraph.

→
Phase 03
📝

Summarization

Each IDAP field is summarised using prompt-engineered OpenAI calls, mapping to the FSMA template sections. Confidence scores are assigned per field.

→
Phase 04
🌐

Translation

Content is translated NL↔FR↔EN by OpenAI, preserving dates, percentages, references and legal names verbatim. Cross-language semantic validation flags deviations.

→
Phase 05
🔗

Delivery

Validated IDAP content is pushed to DCC for final document generation and storage. Full traceability report and audit log generated automatically.

Our approach

Built for auditability and scale

Our architecture goes beyond extraction — every decision is traceable, every output is governed.

01

Section-by-section traceability

Every generated sentence maps back to the exact source document, version, page, section and paragraph. Zero hallucination risk on content origin.

02

Confidence scoring per field

Each IDAP field carries a confidence score based on semantic match strength. Low-confidence items are automatically flagged for business review.

03

Knowledge base with versioning

Postgres SQL + pgvector stores chunked documents with scheme ID, language, effective dates and embeddings — so the system always knows which rules applied when.

04

Semantic search frontend

A Streamlit-based UI lets business users search across all pensioenreglementen, view error logs, access traceability reports and monitor processing status.

Governance & risk

AI Act-ready, by design

We positioned the IDAP agent as a document-drafting assistant — not a decision-making system. Every risk is mitigated with concrete architectural and process controls.

🤖

Hallucination risk

Medium → Low

The agent works with fixed FSMA templates and structured section keys. Every output must show its source reference. Low-confidence fields are flagged for expert review before any document is finalized.

⚖️

EU AI Act classification

Low
